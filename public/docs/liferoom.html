LifeRoom Clients & Sandboxes

Local installs live in Apps/LifeRoom (ClassiCube / Roblox / Second Life / Luanti / OpenSim). This page gives quick run pointers.

Locations

  • ClassiCube client: Apps/LifeRoom/ClassiCube
  • Roblox Player / Studio installers: Apps/LifeRoom/Roblox*
  • Second Life viewer installer: Apps/LifeRoom/Second_Life_7_2_2_18475198968_x86_64_Setup.exe
  • Luanti (Minetest fork): Apps/LifeRoom/luanti-5.14.0-win64
  • OpenSim: Apps/LifeRoom/opensim-0.9.3.0

How to Use

  1. Open the folder above on your machine.
  2. Run the installer or executable for the desired client.
  3. Sign in / join your preferred LifeRoom world server.
